Fuzzy Navel

Meet the Fuzzy Navel. Donâ€™t be turned off by the bizarre name, which is admittedly not the most appetizing. Before your mind wanders to grooming habits, the explanation is quite simple: Fuzzy refers to the peach schnapps (think peach fuzz) and the navel refers to the type of orange.

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces peach schnapps
  • 3 ounces orange juice, freshly squeezed
  • Garnish: 1 orange slice

Steps

  1. Add the peach schnapps and orange juice into a highball glass over ice, and stir briefly to combine.
  2. Garnish with an orange slice.
